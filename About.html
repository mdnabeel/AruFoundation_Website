About Organization


As a rapidly urbanizing nation, it is estimated that nearly 300 million people are now living in cities in India. Urban poverty and destitution is rising at a directly proportional rate owing to an unplanned model of urbanization that demands an increased availability of casual and unorganized labour, while failing to develop the most basic housing and sanitation amenities for migrant and poor communities that throng urban settlements in search for a better life. ARU Foundation is working on ground from past 2 years with vulnerable children on an individual Basis, than group came together to extend their support to women in different areas of Delhi by Interacting with them to understand the needs.
ARU foundation is a development organization registered as a not for profit society as on 29th December 2017. It is a non- political,non-religious organization. Its mission is to unite with people in India order to create development in the broadcast sense through the implementation of the projects, which aims in transferring knowledge skills and capacity to individuals and communities who need assistance to live in a society which is free from poverty.

Who are Vulnerable


In every segment of population there are several degrees of vulnerability based on the demographics of the person, what their existing social relationships are, andwhether the happen to be male, female or children. In our experience, women aged 16-35 and children under 16 continue to outrank all others in terms of how vulnerable they remain while they are on the streets, or in conditions where they don't have basic amenities.The roof over their heads is which not properly made.
A common tale that unites their experiences is one of intense vulnerability that leaves women particularly prone to exploitation and severe abuse. Absolute poverty and starvation thrust many into a pit of helplessness, where the added responsibility of feeding young children often leads both mother and child to perish.
Vulnerability is a vast term, Individual can be vunerable in many ways, the people living in the place is vulnerable because they are migrated from different states and living in conditions where they don't have access to basic amenities like electricity, clean water etc. They were exploited by Zamindar's on whose land they used to cultivate on a yearly basis contract.


About Target Area


Chilla Khadar is among the handful of villages on the floodplains of Yamuna in Delhi where the main occupation is farming. The places is lack in basic amenities like Pucca road, School, anganwadi, electricity, clean drinking water and Toilets, despite they have a population of 2000 Families. Most of the families are living in this condition from past 30 years, as majority of the population is migrated from Uttar pradesh and Bihar.
Chilla khaddar is considered as unauthorized colony in Delhi and for most people it is a village in Delhi which is on Farming land and most of the facility doesn't reach to them because of the farming or agricultural land they have. Another area in which we are working is Okhla, where our focus is to develop community and link them with governmental schemes.
