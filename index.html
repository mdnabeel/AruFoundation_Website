About Aru


ARU foundation is a development organization registered as a not for profit society as on 29th December 2017. It is a non- political, non-religious organization. Its mission is to unite with people in India in order to create development in the broadcast sense through the implementation of the projects, which aims in transferring knowledge skills and capacity to individuals and communities who need assistance to live in a society which is free from poverty.
